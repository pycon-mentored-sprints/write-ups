Mentored sprints for diverse beginners online: May 16-17th 2020

Thanks for volunteering as a mentor!


We are very excited to be running this sprint as part of the PyCon online 2020 activities. And we are equally grateful to you for volunteering, this would not be possible without you all.

With the event moving online we had to make some changes and adjustments to ensure everyone is able to participate and make the most of the sprints.

What you need to know

The event will start on Saturday 16th May at 9:00 PDT and finish on Sunday 17th May at 13:00 JST.

We have two slots for participation:

  • Americas + Europe + Africa friendly slot: 9:00 PDT | 11:00 CDT | 17:00 BST | 19:00 EAT Check the event time in your timezone
  • APAC friendly slot: 9:00 JST | 10:00 AEST Check the event time in your timezone

We have created a Discord server for the event. This will be used to split attendees and mentors based on projects as well as providing general help.

Use this link to join the server:
